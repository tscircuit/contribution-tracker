- Core PCB engine integrates jumper autorouting and improves async footprint/layout handling ([[tscircuit/core #1821](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1821)](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1821), [#1819](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1819), [#1820](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1820))
