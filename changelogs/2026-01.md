# Changelog 2026-01

- PCB autorouting leaps forward
  – Jump-to-jumper routing: first working jumper autorouter (#510), reduced channel points & 6×4 solver (#520), high-density/ GameBoy fixes (#512, #521), unused-jumper filter (#524) and curvy-trace solver integration with Pipeline 3 enabled (#528, #529)
  – Quality tweaks: straight-line deviation penalty (#506), multi-section/pathing parameterization (#511, #517) and port-point rip-threshold & effort controls (#530, #1834, #1833)

- Core platform & layout
  – Full jumper-routing integration into core net connectivity (#1821, #1824, #1826)
  – Async-footprint & PCB-note layout fixes (#1819, #1827)
  – AutorouterVersion / auto-jumper preset support (#1831, props #551-552)
  – New <schematicpath/> element with SVG support (core #1835, c-to-svg #479)
  – Solder-mask conflict warnings emitted at build (#1829)

- Rendering stack upgrades
  – circuit-to-canvas adds rotation, solder-mask margins, knockout text & copper-pour BREP support (#104, #108, #106, #112)
  – pcb-viewer migrates to primitive-level/ via/ copper-pour rendering & hover highlights (#595, #604)
  – 3d-viewer gains STEP→GLB fallback, pill/oval hole handling & polygon-pad solder-mask clearance (#638, #647, #651, #655)
  – circuit-to-svg improves keep-out patterning & viewport control (#477-478)

- Autorouter math / geometry engines
  – hypergraph introduces gradient-descent & serialization, plus bounds/jumper enhancements (#5, #15, #20)
  – rectdiff resolves stalling with complex polygons (#65)
  – curvy-trace-solver adds space-easing & cross-net-intersection penalty (#9, #2)

- CLI & workflow conveniences
  – New buildCommand / prebuildCommand and --ci flag (#1570, #1575, #1577)
  – push command gains upload summary, --include-dist option & better error naming (#1568, #1565, #1572)
  – Always-use-latest-tscircuit cloud option and binary-asset clone fixes (#1617, #1629)

- Converters & libraries
  – EasyEDA converter now parses pin labels with ‘#’ (#341) and runs in parallel (#342)
  – New KicadLibraryConverter API w/ user vs builtin paths (c-json-to-kicad #47) and default-export support (#49)
  – GLTF export supports panel cut-outs (#108); LBRN export merges plated-hole pads with traces (#86)

- Application UX polish
  – pcbburn.com: loading/error states, live-settings, SVG auto-fit & context-menu disable (#17, #20-22, #25-26)
  – tscircuit.com: revamped builds/releases pages, github-sync & mobile-friendly repo view (#2441-2447, #2451-2455, #2470-2476)

- Miscellaneous
  – Footprinter gains convex/concave pad flags, resistor-array naming improvements & mounted-module tests (#470-476, #480)
  – Math-utils, props and common packages receive new geometry helpers and preset support (#28-31, common #64)
