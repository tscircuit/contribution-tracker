# Changelog 2025-12

- Copper text everywhere – Added pcb_copper_text with knockout support across PCB Viewer (#525), 3D Viewer (#617), Circuit-to-SVG (#442), and Gerber generator (#74)
- PCB Viewer improvements – Pill-pad rotation fix (#523) and baseline-aligned text (#515)
- 3D Viewer overhaul – Solder-mask & cut-out accuracy, new texture layer rendering (#614), transparency support (#589), fabrication-note rectangles (#597), and JSCAD trace bug fix (#618)
- Panelization & packing – Re-wrote tab/mouse-bite logic (#1729), added tab-routing for custom outlines (#1728), static-component packing (#1730), grid-packing refactor (#1736), and lifecycle events/error reporting (#1734)
- Autorouter gains – Net-merge & route-simplification engines (#364 / #366), multilayer connection handling (#362), board-outline awareness (#386), and new high-density examples (#387 / #390)
- Core SPICE upgrades – Correct inductance handling and buck-converter example support (#1727); improved MOSFET models in circuit-json-to-spice (#28–30); enforced tstep in ngspice-engine (#6)
- Board-outline support pushed down-stack – rectdiff (#20) plus via-as-pad handling in calculate-packing (#84)
- Polygon & pill SMT pad support – circuit-json-util (#71) with matching viewer fixes (#517) and solder-mask coverage fixes
- Panel/board props – New <fiducial />, <subpanel />, layout modes & boardGap (#526); removed redundant panel dims (#527)
- GLTF converter – Translucent model rendering support (#96) used by runframe (#2049)
- Footprinter – Correct SOT-23 pin-1 indicator & parity test (#438/439)
- CLI – KiCad footprint-library export support (#1254) and improved image export & caching tweaks
- Misc. converters – Multi-layer copper in circuit-json-to-lbrn (#48); DSN ⇄ Circuit-JSON pipelines (#1-10); via route_type dedup in dsn-converter (#117)
- Documentation – New panel/board, filter, translucent-model and SPICE example docs (#362-377)
- Quality fixes – z-fighting (#611), color errors (#610), pill-hole rotations (#600), and many solder-mask edge-cases (#595-606)
