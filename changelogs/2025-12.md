# Changelog 2025-12

- Copper text support end-to-end   • PCB viewer renders copper text with knockout option – [pcb-viewer #525](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/525)   • 3-D viewer shows copper text with knockout & mirroring – [3d-viewer #617](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/617)   • SVG generator gains full copper-text export – [circuit-to-svg #442](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-svg/pull/442)   • Canvas output and Gerber files now include copper text – [circuit-to-canvas #6](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-canvas/pull/6), [circuit-json-to-gerber #74](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json-to-gerber/pull/74)
- New anchor-offset & relative-positioning system   • Groups/boards can be positioned relative to other groups/boards with visual overlay – [pcb-viewer #539](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/539), [#537](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/537)   • SVG visualises group-to-group offsets – [circuit-to-svg #449](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-svg/pull/449)   • Core adds `position_mode` metadata and edge-padding props – [core #1751](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1751), [#1740](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1740)
- Panelisation & board-outline improvements   • Tab-routing rewritten for custom outlines – [core #1729](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1729), [#1728](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1728)   • 3-D viewer now draws board outlines inside panels – [3d-viewer #619](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/619)
- New PCB primitives   • `CopperText` primitive added to Core – [core #1760](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1760)   • `Fiducial` primitive/component introduced – [core #1765](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1765), [props #527](https://github.com/tscircuit/props/pull/527)
- Fabrication-note graphics   • 3-D viewer renders fabrication-note rectangles & lines – [3d-viewer #597](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/597), [#621](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/621)
- Voltage-probe labels   • Automatic label alignment & SVG overflow fix – [core #1756](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1756), [circuit-to-svg #455](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-svg/pull/455)
- SMT pad reliability   • Correct rotation for pill-shaped SMT pads – [pcb-viewer #523](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/523)
- Footprint additions & fixes   • Proper Pin 1 indicator for SOT-23 – [footprinter #439](https://github.com/tscircuit/footprinter/pull/439)   • New SOT-343-4 footprint – [footprinter #440](https://github.com/tscircuit/footprinter/pull/440)
- SPICE enhancements   • Better inductance handling & buck-converter support – [core #1727](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1727)
- Miscellaneous UX tweaks   • Right-click disabled in PCB viewer – [pcb-viewer #518](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/518)   • Group centre markers changed to plus sign – [pcb-viewer #541](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/541)   • Text baseline alignment corrected – [pcb-viewer #515](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/515), [circuit-to-canvas #18](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-canvas/pull/18)
