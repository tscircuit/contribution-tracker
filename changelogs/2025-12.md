# Changelog 2025-12

- Added full copper text rendering (normal & knockout) in both PCB Viewer and SVG output, scaled to match silkscreen – [pcb-viewer #525](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/525), [circuit-to-svg #442](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-svg/pull/442), [circuit-to-svg #444](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-svg/pull/444)
- PCB SVGs now show panel anchor-offset indicators – [circuit-to-svg #441](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-svg/pull/441)
- Fixed rotation support for pill-shaped SMT pads across viewers – [pcb-viewer #523](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/523), [3d-viewer #600](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/600)
- On-board text now aligns with the alphabet baseline – [pcb-viewer #515](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/515)
- Right-click context menu disabled inside PCB Viewer to prevent unwanted browser actions – [pcb-viewer #518](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/518)
- Fixed panel center-position calculation in PCB Viewer – [pcb-viewer #520](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/520)
- New panel features: <fiducial/>, <subpanel/>, additional layout modes & boardGap setting – [props #526](https://github.com/tscircuit/props/pull/526)
- Re-implemented tab routing & mouse-bite generation and added support for custom-outline boards – [core #1729](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1729), [core #1728](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1728)
- Panelization defaults to “none” and respects user-defined board size/position – [core #1722](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1722), [core #1719](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1719), [core #1720](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1720)
- 3D Viewer gains texture-layer rendering, optional soldermask visibility and model transparency – [3d-viewer #614](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/614), [3d-viewer #595](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/595), [3d-viewer #589](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/589)
- Numerous 3D rendering fixes for soldermask coverage, cutouts, plated/non-plated holes & rotations – e.g. [#604](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/604), [#606](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/606), [#602](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/602), [#603](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/603), [#598](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/598)
- Corrected SOT-23 footprint geometry and pin-1 indicator – [footprinter #438](https://github.com/tscircuit/footprinter/pull/438), [footprinter #439](https://github.com/tscircuit/footprinter/pull/439)
- Autorouter adds configurable iterative trace-simplification solver and net-merging for electrically connected traces – [autorouter #366](https://github.com/tscircuit/tscircuit-autorouter/pull/366), [autorouter #364](https://github.com/tscircuit/tscircuit-autorouter/pull/364)
- Board-outline awareness introduced to geometry libs & packing – [rectdiff #20](https://github.com/tscircuit/rectdiff/pull/20), [calculate-packing #84](https://github.com/tscircuit/calculate-packing/pull/84)
- Bounds/transform utilities now handle polygon SMT pads – [circuit-json-util #71](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json-util/pull/71)
- SPICE improvements: better MOSFET models, fixed inductance handling and buck-converter support – [core #1727](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1727), [circuit-json-to-spice #30](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json-to-spice/pull/30), [#29](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json-to-spice/pull/29), [#28](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json-to-spice/pull/28)
- CLI enhancements: export to KiCad footprint library, reliable kicad_mod uploads, resilient preview-image generation and downgraded missing-main error to warning – [cli #1254](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/1254), [cli #1240](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/1240), [cli #1270](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/1270), [cli #1290](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/1290)
