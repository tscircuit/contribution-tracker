# Changelog 2026-05

Key highlights for May 2026 (selection of most impactful PRs)

Autorouting & Routing Phases
- Introduced “autorouting phase” workflow – add, reroute, unroute and rectangular phase support (core #2236, #2249, #2260, #2245, #2256)
- Added shape/tolerance/DRC-param props and krt-wasm preset for phases (props #659-#661, core #2267, eval #2596)
- Capacity-autorouter bumped to v0.0.505+ with via-to-pad clearance solver (core #2241, autorouter #1113)

- Top/Bottom courtyard & plated-hole rotation fixes in footprint / KiCad exporters (cj-to-tscircuit #30, cj-to-kicad #294)

Design-rule Checking / Repair
- Polygon-aware DRC for rotated pads & plated holes (checks #143)

Viewer / CLI / Tooling

Other Notables
