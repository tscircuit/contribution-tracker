# Changelog 2026-05

Key enhancements & fixes delivered in May 2026

- Introduced “autorouting phase” workflow
  – Rectangular & component-scoped phases, default phase injection (core #2249 #2236 #2260)
  – Phase‐level tolerances, shape & DRC parameter support (props #659-#660, core #2267)
  – Platform-selectable autorouter function + KRT-wasm preset (core #2256 #2303, props #661, eval #2596)

- PCB feature robustness
  – Via inflation & preserved pcb_trace import (core #2278 #2262)
  – New through_pad route type with SVG/GERBER support (c-json #579, svg #560, gerber #89)
  – Blind-/buried-via, rotated pill/oval hole & plated-hole fixes (core #2250, c-json-to-kicad #294, gerber #88)
  – Top/Bottom courtyard & courtyard circle generation (c-json-to-tsc #30 #32)

- Conversion pipeline upgrades
  – KiCad 10 support, zone properties, full via graph & pcb_trace grouping (kicad-to-c-json #74 #80-#86; kicadts #35 #37 #39 #40)
  – Import kicad_pcb as Board component & boardContent export (eval #2574 #2595)

- Design-rule checking & repair
  – Polygon-aware DRC for rotated pads/holes + new clearance checks (checks #143 #147 #149)
  – Accurate missing-trace endpoint & multi-pad port handling (checks #144 #145)
  – High-density-repair03 DRC14 benchmark & interactive debugger (hdr03 #11-#17)

- Footprint & props improvements
  – Parsed footprinter strings, JLC footprint autocomplete & hinting (core #2263 #2301; props #671)
  – Resistor footprint mapping, optional port name/direction, nominalTraceWidth field (props #657-#669)

- Tooling & UX
  – CLI static-file loading for KiCad imports (cli #2987)
  – PCB-viewer “Bounds” rectangle selection tool (pcb-viewer #864)
  – Order-dialog CSS isolation to avoid host clashes (order-dialog #1)

- Documentation & examples
  – Docs for autorouting phases, group schematic boxes, local STEP models (docs #549-#553)

- Library & board additions
  – SparkFun MAX31855K, RJ11 & DE9 breakouts; Arduino Uno, BGA & other repro datasets
