# Changelog 2026-03

- Subpanels are now fully respected: group overlays are hidden, nested boards are panelized, and grid layouts support subpanels ([pcb-viewer #687](https://github.com/tscircuit/pcb-viewer/pull/687), [core #2005](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2005), [core #2002](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2002))
- Platform-wide flags can disable individual or all DRC checks via platformConfig ([props #608](https://github.com/tscircuit/props/pull/608), [props #611](https://github.com/tscircuit/props/pull/611), [core #2003](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2003), [core #2017](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2017))
- Connector updates: added simple_connector spec, optional cable insertion center, inward-facing connector warning, and new accessibility placement check ([circuit-json #487](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json/pull/487), [circuit-json #489](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json/pull/489), [circuit-json #491](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json/pull/491), [checks #107](https://github.com/tscircuit/checks/pull/107))
- pcb_component anchors are now optional and may be positioned relative to another component ([circuit-json #485](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-json/pull/485))
- CLI improvements: pseudo-orthographic camera preset, multipart binary uploads, --inject-props flag, better defaults, libraryEntrypoint support, and build failure fix on warnings ([cli #2232](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/2232), [cli #2229](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/2229), [cli #2239](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/2239), [cli #2269](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/2269), [cli #2263](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/2263), [cli #2275](https://github.com/tscircuit/cli/pull/2275))
- Visualization: context-menu toggle for pcb_note elements, improved plated-hole and panel-outline textures, showPcbNotes option, and accurate copper clipping at drills/vias ([3d-viewer #721](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/721), [3d-viewer #724](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/724), [3d-viewer #723](https://github.com/tscircuit/3d-viewer/pull/723), [circuit-to-canvas #202](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-canvas/pull/202), [circuit-to-canvas #204](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-canvas/pull/204), [circuit-to-canvas #206](https://github.com/tscircuit/circuit-to-canvas/pull/206))
- Autorouter upgrades: faster obstacle checks, higher iteration cap, nominal trace-width support, and deterministic via-tile topology ([autorouter #619](https://github.com/tscircuit/tscircuit-autorouter/pull/619), [autorouter #621](https://github.com/tscircuit/tscircuit-autorouter/pull/621), [core #2019](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2019), [hypergraph #121](https://github.com/tscircuit/hypergraph/pull/121))
- New JST ZH connector footprint supported by Footprinter ([footprinter #514](https://github.com/tscircuit/footprinter/pull/514))
- Easier trace & symbol selectors: Component.pin shorthand, implicit single-pin selectors, and automatic footprint port extraction ([core #2007](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2007), [core #2010](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2010), [core #2008](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2008))
- Clearer diagnostics: missing-footprint error, better pin-label error, and improved overlap messages ([core #1998](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1998), [core #1997](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/1997), [checks #106](https://github.com/tscircuit/checks/pull/106))
- Schematic auto-layout now works with custom symbols defined via schematic_path ([core #2015](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2015))
- Prevented PCB trace render crashes on unresolved selectors ([core #2012](https://github.com/tscircuit/core/pull/2012))
