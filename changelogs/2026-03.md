# Changelog 2026-03

- Sub-panel & panelization support: core now ignores sub-panels in group layouts and includes nested boards; viewer hides group overlay on sub-panels (#2005, #2002, pcb-viewer#687).
- Platform-wide DRC control: added platformConfig flags to selectively or fully disable DRC checks (#608, #2003, props#611).
- Connector workflow: new simple_connector spec, <connector/> element, automatic cable-insert center and inward-facing orientation warning (#487, core#2011, circuit-json#491, checks#107).
- Clearer DRC output: improved missing-footprint and invalid-pinLabel errors plus richer PCB-overlap messages (#1998, #1997, checks#106).
- pcbSx.visibility & fabrication-note toggles, exposed in 2D/3D renderers (#609, core#2016, 3d-viewer#721).
- 3D/Canvas rendering revamp: refactored texture generation for traces, pads, plated holes, panel outlines; per-layer trace splitting & fallback CAD URLs (#723-730, circuit-to-canvas#204‒206, 3d-viewer#728-729).
- CLI upgrades: pseudo-orthographic cameras, concurrent GLB conversion & snapshots, binary upload in sync, --inject-props, libraryEntrypoint, tsci check netlist, tsci search --json (#2232, #2233, #2229, #2239, #2263, #2279, #2282, #2277).
- Autorouter & topology speed-ups: Flatbush obstacle checks, via-tile auto-selection & baking, new datasets, solver-parameter tuning (#619, hypergraph#112, #121-125, autorouter#621, #626).
- Checks pipeline cleanup: removed self-pad-overlap check and moved trace connectivity into routing checks (#105, #110).
- Footprinter: new JST ZH, SOT-223-5, TO-220F-3 footprints with alias support (#514, #515).
- Circuit-JSON extensions: optional anchors & relative positioning, cable_insertion_center field, missing pin attr fix (#485, #489, #493).
- Utilities: getBoardBounds helper & board-bounds calc fix (#81, util#83), KiCad-mod cache 404 fix (#14).
- Fabrication: dedicated top/bottom soldermask layers and 2-layer mask support in LBRN/pcbburn outputs (#149, pcbburn.com#79).
